Business Card Reader is an Android Application. It takes the photo of the paper based visiting card through the camera of the android device and performs scanning of the photo.

Through Optical Character Recognition, the photo is translated into editable text. We can save the photo from the application. It than extracts the name, address, contact info, email id and other description of the card and saves it to the contact list. Our application can be installed on android devices only.
