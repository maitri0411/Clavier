Our Projects





Orbit Agri Remedies is an informative website where the company is about pesticides and insecticides manufactutrer. The website is about the details of various products and its features and the various components involved in the products. The website is designed in quite a user friendly manner and the users can easily know about the products.


IELTS Writing is an Android based app and it is specially designed for the "writing section" which allows IELTS learner to study on their own in a simple, effective and convienient way anywhere, anytime. This app contains 100+ essays, 50+ letters and 20+ graphs. You can also practice on your own by reffering "Practice yourself" section. Take our practice test and find out how well prepared you are. This app also contains some basic tips about the exam which will help you to plan your study and it'll bring more accuracy in your study. All essays, letters and graphs are ideal for General training module as well as Academic module.


Business Card Reader is an Android Application. It takes the photo of the paper based visiting card through the camera of the android device and performs scanning of the photo. Through Optical Character Recognition, the photo is translated into editable text. We can save the photo from the application. It than extracts the name, address, contact info, email id and other description of the card and saves it to the contact list. Our application can be installed on android devices only.


The application is based on the changed tax slabs by the government. Related application provides only partial information. "GST India" is one such application which only gives information regarding GST but fails to calculate tax. "GST All Products Rate Finder" contains items sorted by the tax slabs but it does not provide other functionality to users. "GST Rate Finder" is also a similar application. Our application covers the drawbacks of all such application by providing users various features.
This application is for personal use of user in a single device.The main feature of our application is to find the GST tax based on different tax slabs. The application provides login facility. It is also easy to use. Users can also give feedback so that application can be made more user-friendly. It works on the concept of ‘All in One Place’. Functionality for saving the tax report is also provided.
